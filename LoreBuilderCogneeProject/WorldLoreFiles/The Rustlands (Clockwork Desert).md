- **Setting:** Oros is an endless desert blanketed in red iron dust, littered with the skeletal ruins of ancient, colossal machinery.
    
- **Environment:** Natural water is practically non-existent; moisture must be extracted from deep underground vapor vents. All technology is strictly mechanical, powered by giant clockwork springs, gears, and manual winding stations, as electronic components rust instantly in the atmosphere.
    
- **Faction A (The Gearsmiths):** A monastic order of engineers who control the great winding towers and hoard the knowledge of ancient machine schematics.
    
- **Faction B (The Sand-Scavengers):** Nomadic tribes who roam the dunes on wind-powered land-sails, scavenging scrap metal and fighting for control of water vents.
    
- **Core Rule:** Leaving machinery unwound for more than 24 hours causes the gears to permanently seize due to iron-dust accumulation.
