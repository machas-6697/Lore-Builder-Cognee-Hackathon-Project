- **Setting:** A world composed entirely of thousands of fragmented stone landmasses floating perpetually above an ocean of toxic, impenetrable green mist.
    
- **Environment:** Travel between islands is conducted exclusively via wooden airships powered by solar sails. The islands remain airborne due to deposits of a glowing mineral called Aetherite embedded in the bedrock.
    
- **Faction A (The Sky-Navigators):** A powerful guild of captains who control the trading maps, shipping lanes, and airship manufacturing yards.
    
- **Faction B (The Crystal Monks):** A reclusive religious sect residing on the highest peaks, dedicated to preserving the natural Aetherite deposits from over-mining.
    
- **Core Rule:** If too much Aetherite is mined from a single floating island, the island loses its buoyancy and plummets into the toxic mist below.
