- **Setting:** An ancient, dense valley where the trees, plants, and soil are made entirely of razor-sharp, dark obsidian glass.
    
- **Environment:** The forest is a single, semi-conscious organism. The trees communicate through vibrations running through a massive, interconnected underground root network. Sunlight reflecting off the leaves creates dazzling, blinding light patterns during midday.
    
- **Faction A (The Spore-Keepers):** An order of druids who have learned to walk the grove without breaking the glass, using specialized resin footwear. They harvest the glowing, medicinal fungi that grow on the roots.
    
- **Faction B (The Iron Knights):** An invading military force attempting to clear-cut the forest to harvest the obsidian for weapons, using heavy iron machinery.
    
- **Core Rule:** Introducing raw, unrefined iron into the grove triggers an immediate defensive vibration from the trees, causing the obsidian leaves to shatter and shower the area in glass shards.
