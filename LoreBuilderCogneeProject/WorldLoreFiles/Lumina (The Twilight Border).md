- **Setting:** A tidally locked planet where one half is a scorching, eternal desert of blinding light, and the other half is a frozen wasteland of absolute darkness. The city of Lumina is built exactly on the twilight equator.
    
- **Environment:** Light is not just illumination; it is a physical, harvestable liquid resource. Shadows are not just absences of light; they are semi-conscious entities that mimic their casters.
    
- **Faction A (The Solar Aristocracy):** Elite nobles who live on the edge of the day-side. They hoard liquid light in massive glass reservoirs and control the city's wealth.
    
- **Faction B (The Umbral Syndicate):** Outcasts who operate in the freezing night-side. They have learned to physically detach their shadows and send them into the city to spy and steal.
    
- **Core Rule:** If a shadow remains detached from its human caster for more than 12 hours, it goes feral, becoming a hostile, tangible monster that attempts to kill its original owner.
