- **Setting:** A sprawling, toxic shantytown built entirely inside the skeletal ribcage of a god-sized, dormant biological entity known as The Leviathan.
    
- **Environment:** There is no metal or stone. Buildings are carved from calcified bone and hardened cartilage. The air is thick with pheromone-spores. The primary fuel source is raw, combustible marrow extracted from the beast's bones.
    
- **Faction A (The Marrow-Barons):** Ruthless industrialists who control the massive drilling rigs that extract marrow from the Leviathan's spine. They hold a monopoly on heat and power.
    
- **Faction B (The White Cell Order):** Fanatical zealots who worship the Leviathan. They sabotage the drilling rigs, believing the beast will eventually wake and consume the city.
    
- **Core Rule:** Drilling too deep into a fresh bone layer triggers the Leviathan's autonomous immune system, spawning mindless, lethal antibody-monsters that hunt anything generating heat.
