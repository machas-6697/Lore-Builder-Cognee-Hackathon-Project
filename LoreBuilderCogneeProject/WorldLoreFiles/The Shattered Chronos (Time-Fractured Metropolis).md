- **Setting:** A sprawling Victorian-era city that was shattered by a massive temporal bomb. The city is now divided into strict, invisible borders called Time Zones.
    
- **Environment:** In the Red Zones, time moves 10x slower. In the Blue Zones, time moves 10x faster. Ammunition fired from a Blue Zone stops mid-air when it hits a Red Zone.
    
- **Faction A (The Pendulum Syndicate):** A criminal cartel that smuggles perishable goods and fugitives across temporal borders, utilizing specialized pocket-watches that temporarily stabilize local time.
    
- **Faction B (The Static Guard):** The city's authoritarian police force. They wear heavy brass chronosuits to patrol the borders and prevent time-paradoxes.
    
- **Core Rule:** Crossing a time border without a stabilized chronosuit causes severe physical shock; spending one subjective day in a Blue Zone physically ages a person by two weeks.
