- **Setting:** Aqualume is a sprawling underwater metropolis built inside a subterranean oceanic trench, illuminated entirely by high-voltage bioluminescent neon grids.

- **Environment:** The atmospheric pressure outside the domes is lethal. Citizens live in localized underwater sectors connected by pressurized glass transit tubes. Oxygen is a highly regulated corporate utility and serves as the primary black-market currency.

- **Faction A (The Trench Corps):** The authoritarian corporate military that controls the primary oxygen filtration plants and enforces sector lockdowns.

- **Faction B (The Drift Outlaws):** A rebellious network of deep-sea divers and hackers who steal raw oxygen from corporate pipelines to distribute to the lower-sector slums.

- **Core Rule:** Any rupture in a sector wall triggers an automatic quarantine, trapping everyone inside to prevent total city decompression.
