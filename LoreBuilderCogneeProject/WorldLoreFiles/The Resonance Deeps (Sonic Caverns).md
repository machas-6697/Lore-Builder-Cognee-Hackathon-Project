- **Setting:** An abyssal, subterranean cave system completely devoid of natural light. The entire civilization is built around the manipulation of sound and vibration.
    
- **Environment:** Navigation, communication, and combat are entirely echolocation-based. Weapons do not fire bullets; they emit concentrated kinetic soundwaves. The cavern walls are lined with fragile, explosive resonance crystals.
    
- **Faction A (The Choral Architects):** The ruling class of engineers who "sing" at precise harmonic frequencies to shape the rock and build structures without tools.
    
- **Faction B (The Hush):** A terrifying guild of assassins who utilize forbidden, absolute sound-canceling technology, allowing them to move and kill in total, unnatural silence.
    
- **Core Rule:** Emitting a loud, dissonant frequency (like a scream or an explosion) near the cavern walls shatters the resonance crystals, causing an immediate, catastrophic cave-in.
